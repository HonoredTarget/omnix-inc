About TSA

TSA stands for the “Technology Student Association.” The motto is “Learning to lead in a technical world.” Our chapter definitely lives by this motto every single day. TSA has chapters all over the United States with a common interest in technology. STEM is a field that is in critical need of workers and TSA assists in supplying these workers.

TSA At Chamblee

TSA is a well-known and esteemed club at Chamblee Charter High School. At our chapter, students get their events based on their selections as well as selections made by our teachers. This helps to ensure that everyone is doing something that they love. At Chamblee, TSA has constant meetings and check-ins to ensure that we are always on track to have finished projects by the State Leadership Conference. TSA - The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers Jr (SHPE Jr) at Chamblee Charter HS exist to promote and encourage students to pursue opportunities and experiences in STEM that will help them to build skills in areas such as teamwork, problem solving, critical thinking, project based learning that will equip them to become leaders and productive members of a global technological society.
